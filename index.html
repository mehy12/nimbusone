Overview

NimbusOne --a cutting-edge AI-drone technology designed for rapid urban coverage, optimized for fast delivery, surveillance, or emergency response within a city. Named after the swift and nimble movement of clouds (Nimbus), the drone combines speed, agility, and precision to cover large areas efficiently.
